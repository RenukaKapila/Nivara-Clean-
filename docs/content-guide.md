# Content Guide

Use calm, practical language for non-technical users.

## Preferred Phrases

- "warning signs"
- "pause and verify"
- "ask someone you trust"
- "use the official website or app"
- "Nivara cannot guarantee scam detection"

## Avoid

- "we detect all scams"
- "this is definitely safe"
- "this is definitely a scam"
- "AI-powered protection" unless a real AI provider is active and documented
- technical labels without plain-language explanations

## Recovery Tone

When someone may have clicked, paid, or shared information:

- start with the next safest action
- avoid blame
- say what not to do next
- link to official reporting and recovery resources
